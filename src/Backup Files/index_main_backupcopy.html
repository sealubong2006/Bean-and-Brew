Book a Space

Looking for a cosy corner to relax, catch up, or celebrate? Reserve your space with ease and enjoy the warm atmosphere, delicious brews, and friendly vibes.

Skip the Queue

Why wait in line when your perfect cup can be ready the moment you arrive? Customise your coffee down to the last detail—from bean type to milk preference—and collect it fresh, fast, and exactly how you like it.

More Than Just Coffee

From hearty breakfasts to handcrafted sandwiches, sweet treats to seasonal specials—our menu is packed with flavour. Whether you're craving comfort food or something fresh and vibrant, Bean and Brew serves up more than just your daily caffeine fix. Explore our locally inspired dishes and discover your new favourite bite.

Bake With Us

Discover the joy of baking in our hands-on classes led by passionate local chefs. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned home baker, you'll learn new techniques, create delicious treats, and take home more than just recipes—take home memories.

Build Your Hamper

Mix and match from our selection of handcrafted baked goods to create a hamper that’s perfect for gifting, celebrating, or simply indulging. Choose your favourites, add a personal touch, and let us wrap it up with care.

Find Us Near You

Whether you're strolling through Harrogate, exploring Leeds, or visiting the historic Knaresborough Castle, there's a Bean and Brew waiting to greet you. Each venue offers its own cosy charm, delicious menu, and friendly atmosphere. Find your nearest café and drop by for a moment of calm and a cup of joy.

Stay Connected

Follow us on social media for the latest treats, behind-the-scenes moments, and community highlights. Got something to say? We’d love to hear it. Share your thoughts, feedback, or ideas and help shape the Bean & Brew experience.
