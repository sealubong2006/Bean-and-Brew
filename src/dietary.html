Allergy Information

We take allergens seriously. Here's what to look out for in our products:

Gluten

Found in wheat-based bakes and pastries.

Nuts

Present in some cookies, chocolates, and toppings.

Dairy

Used in milk chocolate, cream fillings, and drinks.

Eggs

Common in pastries, cakes, and some biscuits.

Please check individual product labels or contact us for detailed allergen info.
