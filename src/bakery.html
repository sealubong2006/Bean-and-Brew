Bake with Us

Join our in-house bakers for a hands-on experience that brings the magic of Bean & Brew’s kitchen to life. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned home baker, our lessons are designed to be fun, informative, and deliciously rewarding.
Whats Included:
- Guided baking sessions covering pastries, breads, and seasonal treats.
- All ingredients and tools provided—just bring your enthusiasm.
- Small group sizes for a relaxed, personal experience.
- Take-home goodies baked by you.
Session Details:
Duration: 1.5 to 2 hours per session.
Locations: Leeds, Harrogate, and Knaresborough Castle cafés.
Price: Starting from £25 per person.
Availability: Weekends and select weekday evenings.
Group bookings & private sessions available

Beginner

Learn the baking basics, such as: mixing, kneading, and baking simple breads and pastries. Perfect for first-timers.

£25/mo

Intermediate

Build on your skills with laminated doughs, piping techniques, and enriched breads like brioche and focaccia.

£35/mo

Advanced

Dive into sourdough, patisserie, and decorative finishes. Ideal for confident bakers ready to elevate their craft.

£45/mo

Seasonal Special

Create holiday favourites like mince pies, gingerbread, and spiced loaves. Offered in November and December.

£60/mo

Kids Workshop

A fun, hands-on session for young bakers aged 6–12. Includes decorating cupcakes and making simple doughs.

£15/mo

Grouped Baking

Bake side by side with someone special. Create treats together, learn new skills, and share a sweet tasting experience.

£50/mo
